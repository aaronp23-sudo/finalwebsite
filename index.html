All About Aaron Phillips

I am a college student at the Univerity of, Nevada Las Vegas. I am a third year graphic design major. I love comic books, movies, shows, collecting vinyls, listening and making music, creating designs for posters, flyers, and videos. I also like to make fun video edits of my favorite movies, characters, and shows. I love to watch and play basketball as well as baseball and football. I work as a Registered Behavioral Technician at Sport Social, and I can't wait for you to see all about me on this website!
